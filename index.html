Fern Pattern - Natural Fractals

Fern patterns demonstrate the concept of fractals in nature, where similar patterns repeat at different scales.

Key Features:

  • Self-similarity at multiple scales
  • Recursive growth patterns
  • Mathematical elegance in natural forms

These patterns are implemented using SVG background elements with ATU brand colors.
